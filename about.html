Epicurrence
The Montues is
an adventure
you don’t want to miss

Built for epic adventures, it’s now time for Epicurrence to open the flood gates for more to experience. It’s time for a new take on larger conferences and with Epicurrence, we’re calling it The Montues.

Located up the mountain at the Ritz Carlton Resort at Northstar in Lake Tahoe California, Epicurrence The Montues guarantees the most epic environment to come and get re-inspired, hang loose with other creatives, and to shred the hill with two full days of snowboarding/skiing.

Here's how it will all go down. We’ll arrive on Sunday evening at the Ritz, hang out, and have a discussion together. Monday and Tuesday morning we’ll regroup for breakfast, have another discussion before hitting the slopes for a full day of snowboarding/skiing (lift tickets provided). We’ll warm up back inside over dinner, and then partake in a couple more discussions at night. Check out will be Wednesday morning by noon. It’s the same spirit of the smaller Epicurrence events at larger scale.

The Montues was built with teams in mind, although individuals are obviously welcomed. If you are a team of 1, 2, 10, 20, or any size — bring the whole crew! Treat The Montues as your epic team getaway to reset, and get reinspired.

The Montues is by invitation only. However, after you purchase your ticket, you become the curator. Each attendee can invite whomever they want to and as many people as they want until all the spots are gone.

For more information follow @dannpetty + @epicurrence on Twitter. If you have any immediate questions or want to talk about partnerships, please contact dann@epicurrence.com.
